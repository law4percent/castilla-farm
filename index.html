About Our Farm

Cultivating excellence with sustainable farming practices since 2016

Nestled in the heart of Brgy. Bog-o, Asturias, Cebu, Castilla Farm stands as a testament to sustainable agriculture and community dedication. We specialize in producing premium coconuts, exotic dragonfruit, fresh bananas, and quality corn using environmentally conscious farming methods.

Our mission extends beyond cultivation ‚Äì we're committed to bridging the gap between local farmers and conscious consumers while promoting eco-friendly agricultural practices that benefit our community and environment.

Our journey began in 2016 with a simple vision: to create a sustainable farming operation that would serve our community for generations. Starting with modest corn and banana crops alongside poultry, we've grown into a diverse agricultural enterprise. Today, we're proud to offer coconuts, dragon fruits, and other premium crops, all while maintaining our commitment to quality and sustainability.
